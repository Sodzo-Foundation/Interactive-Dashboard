The Lawuna Project

  • Goal: To establish a freshwater contaminant monitoring system and empower communities with rich, open environmental data.

  • Mission: To improve the quality of water and lives of communities sorrounding fresh water bodies by use of conversational technology through detecting, identifying and monitoring contaminants thus creating a sustainable and friendly ecosystem.

  • Vision: To have free contaminant fresh water bodies.
